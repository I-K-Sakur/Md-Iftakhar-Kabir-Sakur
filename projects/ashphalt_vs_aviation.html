About

Asphalt-vs-Aviation

In this game, players can choose to control various units on either the aviation or asphalt side. There are four primary roles:

  • Aviation Player
  • Aviation AI
  • Vehicle Player
  • Vehicle AI

Players have multiple gameplay modes to select from, including:

  • AI vs AI
  • Aviation AI vs Asphalt Player
  • Asphalt AI vs Aviation Player

The objective is to destroy the opponent. Note that in AI vs AI mode, you can observe the AI battle but cannot interact directly. In player-controlled modes, you can press the 'Enter' button to fire rockets at the enemy. If the enemy's health level drops to zero, they will be destroyed, and you will win the game. However, the same can happen to you, so stay alert!

Additional Settings

Press the 'C' button to change the camera view, allowing you to switch perspectives and get a broader view of both the aviation and vehicle players. For more information on controls and settings, check the Help option in the game menu.
