Storyline and Characters

Story Overview

Zoro and Asad were on a mission to capture Furizz. While on the mission, they were ambushed by Furizz’s army. Furizz is a cartel leader with an entire army. During the fight, Zoro managed to escape, but Asad was captured. Zoro is now back, searching for Asad to rescue him and defeat Furizz.

Main Characters

  • Captain Zoro: The main character and player.
  • The Furizz: The main villain.
  • Captain Asad: Kidnapped and captured by Furizz and his army. He appears in the "Killing Furizz" mission.

Missions

1. Furizz Camp

In this mission, the player must search for Furizz across multiple camps. Starting in the first camp, the player must eliminate enemy soldiers to survive and complete the search. After thoroughly searching the first camp, the player will drive a military truck to the second camp, following a given path. If Furizz is not found, the player will continue to a third and final camp, all while managing health levels.

2. Regestan Mission

Set in a desert at night, this mission takes place in Furizz’s main camp, where Asad is held. The player must interact with and eliminate enemy soldiers, clearing the way to the main gate of the camp. Inside, the player searches each building for Furizz.

3. The Last Run

Captain Asad will follow the player throughout this mission. The player must reach a rooftop in time to board a spaceship while facing off against enemy soldiers. Failure to catch the spaceship or dying will restart the mission.

4. Destroy The Furizz Camp

Using the spaceship, the player must destroy 15 different buildings in the camp. A congratulations message is shown upon completion.

Side Quests/Events

Different quests are available within each mission. Pressing the "Tab" key will display the current quest objectives.

How to Play

  • Use A, S, W, D keys to control the FPS player.
  • Press Space to jump.
  • Press B to enter the truck.
  • Use A, S, W, D keys to operate the truck.
  • Use Arrow keys to control the spaceship.
  • Press Left Mouse Button to shoot and Middle Mouse Button to change weapons.
  • Press SHIFT + W to sprint.
  • Press Enter to set missile targets, and Left Mouse Button or Right Mouse Button to fire missiles.
