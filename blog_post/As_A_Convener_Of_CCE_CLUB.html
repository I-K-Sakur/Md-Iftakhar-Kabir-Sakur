As The Convener of CCE Club Contest Committee

Published on October 31, 2024

I was the convener of the CCE Club Contest Committee.And during this time we organized programming contest here.

Organizing the events and others

There were 20 teams participated into this contest from different batches of CCE department.And showed their problem solving skills.

We, the entire committee, went from batch to batch encouraging students to form teams and participate in the event.
I prepared the budget for the event, which we later discussed in a meeting before submitting it to the Club President.
Our organization was praised by all the faculty members, and the participants also enjoyed it.
On March 11, we organized the prize-giving ceremony and awarded certificates, crests, and kits to the first, second, and third place winners and others.

"And this is how the contest ended..."
